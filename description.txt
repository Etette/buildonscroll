GreenCertify Smart Contract
Objective: GreenCertify is a decentralized application (dApp) that certifies environmentally-friendly products or processes. It allows organizations to issue and verify certifications on the blockchain, providing transparency and immutability to the certification process.

Features:

Issuing Certifications: Organizations can issue certifications for products or processes.
Verification: Anyone can verify the authenticity of a certification.
Revocation: Certifications can be revoked if necessary.
Historical Data: Track all certifications and their statuses.
